---
title: Quickstart
description: Get your site set up with Tollgate in under 10 minutes.
---

## 1. Create an account

Go to [tollgate.obul.ai](https://tollgate.obul.ai) and sign in with Google.

## 2. Add your site

Click **Add Site** and enter your domain (e.g., `example.com`).

## 3. Verify domain ownership

Add a DNS TXT record to prove you own the domain:

```
_tollgate.example.com  TXT  "tollgate-verify=<your-verification-code>"
```

Click **Verify** in the dashboard once the record propagates.

## 4. Set up the payment subdomain

Add a CNAME record pointing your payment subdomain to the Tollgate gateway:

```
pay.example.com  CNAME  gw.obul.ai
```

## 5. Configure your integration

Add a redirect rule on your CDN or edge platform so that AI bot traffic gets redirected to `pay.yourdomain.com`. 

Choose your platform:
- [Cloudflare](/integrations/cloudflare/)
- [Vercel](/integrations/vercel/)
- [Fastly](/integrations/fastly/)
- [Akamai](/integrations/akamai/)
- [AWS CloudFront](/integrations/aws/)
- [Google Cloud](/integrations/google-cloud/)
- [Azure Front Door](/integrations/azure/)
- [DataDome](/integrations/datadome/)
- [Imperva](/integrations/imperva/)
- [WordPress VIP](/integrations/wordpress-vip/)
- [Arc XP](/integrations/arc-xp/)
- [General (any platform)](/integrations/general/)

## 6. Set pricing

In the dashboard, go to your site's **Pricing** tab. Set a default price per page (in USD). You can also configure path-specific pricing for premium content.

## 7. Connect payouts

Go to the **Payouts** tab and connect your Stripe account. You'll receive USD payouts when your balance exceeds $10.

## Done

AI bots visiting your site will now be redirected to the payment gateway. Bots that support x402 will pay automatically; others will receive a 402 response explaining how to get access.
