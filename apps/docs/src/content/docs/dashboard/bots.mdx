## Bot allowlist

By default, all bots hitting your payment gateway must pay. You can allowlist specific bots to bypass payment entirely.

### Adding allowlist entries

1. Go to your site → **Bots** tab
2. Under **Bot Allowlist**, click **Add Entry**
3. Enter a user-agent pattern (e.g., `Googlebot`, `Bingbot`)

Allowlisted bots will receive your content without going through the payment flow.

## Path exclusions

You can exclude specific URL paths from the payment requirement. Requests to excluded paths pass through without triggering payment.

### Adding path exclusions

1. Under **Path Exclusions**, click **Add Exclusion**
2. Enter the path pattern to exclude (e.g., `/robots.txt`, `/sitemap.xml`, `/public/*`)

### Common exclusions

Consider excluding:
- `/robots.txt` — so bots can still read your crawl directives
- `/sitemap.xml` — so search engine bots can discover your pages
- `/favicon.ico` — no value in charging for favicon requests
- `/public/*` — any intentionally public content
