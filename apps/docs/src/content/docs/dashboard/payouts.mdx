## How payouts work

Bot payments are made in USDC on Base chain. Tollgate collects these payments and converts them to USD payouts via Stripe Connect.

### Payout flow

1. Bots pay USDC on Base → your Tollgate balance increases
2. When your balance exceeds **$10**, a payout is triggered
3. Funds are sent to your connected Stripe account
4. From Stripe, funds transfer to your bank account

## Connecting Stripe

1. Go to your site → **Payouts** tab
2. Click **Connect Stripe Account**
3. Complete the Stripe Connect onboarding flow
4. Once connected, payouts happen automatically

## Payout history

The Payouts tab shows:
- **Pending balance** — accumulated but not yet paid out
- **Payout history** — list of completed payouts with amounts and dates
- **Payment history** — individual bot payments received
