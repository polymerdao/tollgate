## Why Tollgate?

AI bots scrape publisher content at scale. Tollgate lets you charge for that access using the x402 HTTP payment protocol — no middlemen, no invoices, just on-chain micropayments.

## How it works

1. **Bot visits your site** — an AI crawler requests a page on your domain
2. **CDN redirects to payment gateway** — your edge layer detects the bot user-agent and issues a 302 redirect to `pay.yourdomain.com`
3. **Gateway returns HTTP 402** — the Tollgate gateway responds with x402 payment terms (price in USDC, wallet address, payment details)
4. **Bot pays on-chain** — the bot submits a USDC payment on Base and includes the transaction proof in a retry request
5. **Content delivered** — Tollgate verifies the payment on-chain, fetches the content from your origin, and returns it to the bot
